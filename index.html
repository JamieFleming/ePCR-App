CrewMate

					
Your second crew member.

				

			


			

				CrewMate is an ambulance documentation assistance tool designed to help
				crews structure assessments, prompts, safety-netting and handover notes
				more clearly during busy prehospital care.
			


			

				
Clinical Use Notice

				

					CrewMate is an assistance and documentation support tool only. It is
					not a replacement for your licensed ePCR system, local trust policy,
					clinical judgement, senior advice, or the official JRCALC / JRCALC
					Plus guidance.
				

				

					Users remain responsible for checking all entries before submission
					and for ensuring records are full, clear, accurate, completed
					promptly, and handled securely in line with HCPC expectations.
