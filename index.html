CrewMate

Your second crew member.

CrewMate is an ambulance documentation assistance tool designed to help crews structure assessments, prompts, safety-netting and handover notes more clearly during busy prehospital care.

Clinical Use Notice

CrewMate is an assistance and documentation support tool only. It is not a replacement for your licensed ePCR system, local trust policy, clinical judgement, senior advice, or the official JRCALC / JRCALC Plus guidance.

Users remain responsible for checking all entries before submission and for ensuring records are full, clear, accurate, completed promptly, and handled securely in line with HCPC expectations.

Guidance References

  • JRCALC: Use the current official JRCALC or JRCALC Plus app/book for definitive UK ambulance clinical guidance.
    Visit JRCALC
  • NICE: NICE guidance should support, not replace, professional clinical judgement and local pathways.
